Welcome to NALANDA DEGREE

Vision

To emerge as a centre of higher learning and innovative research to meet the challenges of a global society by main streaming the practices that facilitate intellectual, emotional, physical, and cultural growth of students.

Mission

To establish and practice novel strategies of learning which are practical, need based and realistic leading to all-round development of student s which enable them to achieve great academic credentials with human excellence.
